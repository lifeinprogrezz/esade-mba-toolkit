# Formula Cheatsheet — Memorize Cold

## Cost composition (Session 1)
- **Prime cost** = DM + DL + Direct expenses (NO overhead)
- **Conversion cost** = DL + Manufacturing OH
- **Total manufacturing cost** = DM + DL + MOH
- **Product cost** (inventoriable) = DM + DL + MOH (NO selling/admin)

## Cost behaviour (Session 1)
- Variable: Total ↑ proportionally with vol; per-unit constant.
- Fixed: Total constant (relevant range); per-unit ↓ as vol ↑.
- High-low: VC/unit = (High cost − Low cost) / (High − Low activity); FC = Total − (VC/unit × Activity).

## Cost assignment (Session 2)
- **OAR = Budgeted OH ÷ Budgeted activity**
- **OH absorbed = OAR × ACTUAL activity** (NOT budgeted)
- **Under/Over recovery = Absorbed − Actual OH** (positive = over)
- Direct method: drop service-to-service shares, rescale to 100%.
- Step-down: order services, close one at a time.

## CVP (Session 3)
- Contribution/unit = SP − VC
- CM% = Contribution / Sales
- **BE units = FC ÷ Contribution/unit**
- **BE revenue = FC ÷ CM%**
- MoS = Budgeted − BE sales; MoS% = MoS / Budgeted
- Target units = (FC + Target profit) ÷ Contribution/unit
- Target revenue = (FC + Target profit) ÷ CM%
- Multi-product: weighted avg contrib = Σ(Contribᵢ × Mix%ᵢ); combined BE = FC ÷ weighted avg.

## CVP graph
- TC line slope = **VC/unit** (NOT contribution).
- TR line slope = **SP/unit**.
- TC y-intercept = **FC** (a loss at zero volume).
- Vertical gap above BE = profit; below BE = loss.

## Relevant costs (Session 3)
- Relevant if: future + differs + real cash flow.
- Material:
  - Stock, regular use → replacement cost
  - Stock, no other use → resale value (or 0)
  - Must buy → purchase price
- Labour:
  - Spare capacity → 0
  - Fully employed → wages + opportunity cost
  - Overtime → wages + premium

## Decision rules (Session 3)
1. Special order, spare cap: accept if **Price > VC**.
2. Special order, no cap: accept if **Price > VC + Opportunity cost (lost contribution)**.
3. Make-or-buy: buy if **Supplier < VC + Avoidable FC + Opportunity cost** (exclude apportioned).
4. Drop segment: keep if **Contribution > Avoidable FC**.
5. Limiting factor: rank by **Contribution per unit of bottleneck**.

## Budgeting (Session 4) — master cascade
1. Sales: Revenue = Units × SP
2. Production: **Units = Sold + Closing FG − Opening FG** ("end + plus, have − minus")
3. Materials usage: Usage = Production × Material/unit
4. Materials purchases: **Purchases = Usage + Closing RM − Opening RM**
5. Labour: Hours = Production × Hrs/unit; Wages = Hours × Rate
6. Variable OH: = DLH × Var rate
7. Total OH budget rate = Total OH ÷ Total DLH (or driver)
8. Cash: **Opening + Receipts − Payments = Closing**

## Cash budget rules
- Depreciation: **NEVER** appears.
- Capex: **full amount** in month paid (not depreciated).
- Receipts/payments lag = follow stated credit terms.
- Sales recorded P&L when made; cash arrives later.

## Standard costing variances (Session 5)
- SQ = standard quantity **for actual output** (i.e. flexed).
- SH = standard hours **for actual output**.

### Materials
- **MPV = (SP − AP) × AQ** [purchasing]
- **MUV = (SQ − AQ) × SP** [production]

### Labour
- **LRV = (SR − AR) × AH**
- **LEV = (SH − AH) × SR**

### Variable OH
- **VO Exp = (Std rate × AH) − Actual VO**
- **VO Eff = (SH − AH) × Std rate**

### Fixed OH
- **FO Exp = Budgeted FO − Actual FO**
- **FO Volume = FO absorbed − Budgeted FO**, FO absorbed = SH × FOAR
- FO Volume = Capacity + Efficiency:
  - **Capacity = (AH − Budgeted hrs) × FOAR**
  - **Efficiency = (SH − AH) × FOAR**
- Marginal costing → NO volume variance.

### Sales (sign reversed!)
- **Sales Price Var = (AP − SP) × Actual units** (positive = higher price = F)
- **Sales Volume Var = (Actual − Budgeted units) × Std contribution/unit**

## Sign convention
- Cost variances: (Std − Act) × Qty. Positive = F, negative = A.
- Sales price variance: (Actual − Standard) × Units. Higher actual price = F.

## Reconciliation (marginal costing)
```
Budgeted contribution
± Sales volume var
= Flexed budget contribution
± Sales price var
± Material (price + usage)
± Labour (rate + efficiency)
± VO (expenditure + efficiency)
= Actual contribution
− Actual fixed overhead
= Actual profit
```
